DOMINICAN REPUBLIC

It's a beautiful country in the Caribbean, known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and delicious food. The capital is Santo Domingo, and the official language is Spanish. The Dominican Republic is famous for its merengue music and baseball. It's a popular tourist destination with lots of exciting activities to do. third-largest by population, with approximately 10.7 million people in 2022, of whom approximately 3.3 million live in the metropolitan area of Santo Domingo, the capital city. It was founded in 1844 on febuary 27 by juan pablo duarte.
